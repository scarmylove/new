Description

The Supplier and Purchase Order Management System is a computerized information system designed to manage supplier records, purchase orders, and payment transactions.

The system aims to improve procurement efficiency, reduce manual errors, and ensure accurate monitoring of supplier transactions.

It provides automated reports to support decision-making in organizations and ensures transparency in procurement operations.

About Us

The Supplier and Purchase Order Management System (SPOMS) was developed by Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) students from Bohol Island State University Clarin Campus.

The development team is led by Dennis Jr. Lopez together with John Lester Poquita, Angel Rose Cepe, and Jennifer Urboda.

The project was created to design a practical system that helps organizations manage supplier information and purchase orders efficiently.

Through SPOMS, the team demonstrates knowledge in system analysis, software development, and database management while providing a modern solution for procurement processes.
